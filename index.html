Travel & Directions

By Train

The nearest train stations are Kings Lynn and Watlington.

Local taxi firms are: Swaffham Taxis (08006129063); Al's Cabs (01760720002); and Platinum Cars (01760725366).

By Car

For the reception venue, follow signs towards "Westacre Gardens" until you turn off onto a private road. Go straight down this small road, towards some cottages. Follow the road round to the left, in front of some single storey barn conversions, and you'll reach the gates to Home Farm Barn
